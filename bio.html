Galerie Jadel

Art Gallery
Shanghai, China

Jadel is an art gallery located in Shanghai that champions contemporary artists who push their own boundaries, constantly research and develop new materials, work across media and disciplines, and engage with the vital concerns of natural beauty.

We value collaboration, experimentation, and the belief that art makes experiencing other imaginaries possible.

We encourage artists to explore different materials and mediums, and regularly organize material research activities. We firmly believe that nature is a source of inspiration and beauty, providing new creative insights and ideas for diverse audiences.

Gallery Jadel is a polyvalent gallery space, provides a new window and platform for artists with an international vision stewarded by Baohua LUO.
